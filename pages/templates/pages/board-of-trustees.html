Board of Trustees

The Board of Trustees of REDAID NIGERIA consists of the Chairman and four other members of the association. The Board of Trustees is elected for a period of four years and can be re-elected two times. All matters of the association are subject to the resolutions of the Board of Trustees, unless the approval of the General Assembly is required. The Executive Board of Trustees appoints the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of the organization.
